Nagachaitra Engineering Services

Nagachaitra Engineering Services (NCESPL) specialized in Custom Design, manufacturing and servicing of power electronics equipment for Oil & Gas, Energy and Utilities, Industrial process & service, Railways, Telecom, Solar Static Power Converters & Storage Systems. Based in Bengaluru, India with beautiful production space and a large full-testing area, the company can call on more experience and multidisciplinary R&D. NCESPL can provide onsite service and technical support everywhere.

Services

Check our Services

Custom Power Electronics Fabrication

Bespoke design and manufacturing of advanced power systems—UPS, battery chargers, inverters, DC‑DC converters, AC/DC distribution panels, MV/LV switchgear, MCC/PCC, compact substations, VFD & soft‑starter panels and more—for sectors like Oil & Gas, Telecom, Railways, Solar and Utilities.

On‑Site Installation & Commissioning

Complete end‑to‑end setup and commissioning services: UPS, battery banks, LT/HT panels, switchyards, transformers, AC/DC distribution systems, busducts and solar electrification, backed by on-site testing, surveys, and certification.

Preventive Maintenance & Lifecycle Support

Nationwide maintenance programmes with tailored contracts: preventive maintenance, asset lifecycle management, equipment database control, waste disposal, system upgrades/refurbishment, and 24×7 call‑out support by skilled engineers.

Industrial Panel Solutions

Specialised panels engineering for industrial applications: metering cubicles, NGR/LAVT, synchronizing and control panels, busducts, MCC/PCC/PMCC units, compact substations, AC/DC distribution, switchgear & relay systems, all built to high safety and reliability standards.

Battery & Secure Power Systems

High-efficiency industrial battery chargers, turnkey battery bank installations, temporary battery hire, load bank rentals, and SCADA/Modbus-enabled secure power & storage solutions.

Trading & Equipment Supply

Comprehensive sourcing and supply of energy hardware: industrial batteries, UPS/inverter systems, solar panels, DC‑DC converters, cables, transformers, resistors, and switchyard equipment—available on a turnkey basis.
